## 🎨 LIGHTING LANGUAGE NOTES

> These notes assist human photographers interpreting these prompts for real shoots.

| Setting | Light Source | Mood |
|---|---|---|
| Gallery Interior | Late afternoon through glass, west-facing | Warm, directional, long soft shadows |
| Courtyard | Filtered canopy sunlight, overhead | Dappled, organic, high contrast texture |
| Gallery Café | North window light, flat and cool | Quiet, editorial, no drama |
| Detail Shot | Single soft box simulating window | Intimate, texture-forward |

**Key Rule:** No hard flash. No dramatic studio setups. The RL Fold must be revealed by light, not constructed by it.

## 📌 HOW TO USE THESE PROMPTS

1. **Midjourney:** Paste directly into Discord prompt field. Adjust `--ar` for format needs (4:5 for feed, 9:16 for Reels, 1:1 for grid).
2. **DALL-E 3 (ChatGPT):** Paste as your full message. For detail control, add: *"Do not add extra people, props, or backgrounds not described."*
3. **Iteration:** After first output, add `--seed [number]` in Midjourney to lock the composition and vary only color or garment detail.
4. **Real Shoot Brief:** Use Prompt Set B close-ups as your **shot list additions** for any live photographer — these are the angles a human shooter must capture to complete the story.
