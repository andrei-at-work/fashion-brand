### ✦ THE EXECUTIVE PRODUCTION PROMPT ✦

**Role:** Senior Art Director & Executive Editor.
**Project Title:** **"BUILDING A FASHION HOUSE: ROSEMARY LEGOH"**
**Context:** This document is a gift for the designer, serving as a definitive roadmap for the brand’s success. It must feel like a high-fashion monograph (e.g., *Vogue*, *Cereal*, or *Kinfolk*).

#### 1. STRATEGIC NARRATIVE RE-GROUPING
The 18 Power Prompts must be re-ordered into five "Construction Phases." Organize the content as follows:
* **PHASE I: THE SOVEREIGN IDENTITY** (Prompts regarding Naming, Persona, and Community).
* **PHASE II: THE ARCHITECTURAL SIGNATURE** (Prompts regarding Logo Design, the "RL Fold," and Visual Art Direction).
* **PHASE III: MATERIAL INTELLIGENCE** (Prompts regarding Fabric Sourcing, QC, and the "Loom Grid" system).
* **PHASE IV: THE ECONOMICS OF SCARCITY** (Prompts regarding Unit Economics, Inventory Logic, and the Waitlist).
* **PHASE V: THE PERMANENT HOUSE** (Prompts regarding Retention, Digital Strategy, and the Strategic Stress Test).

#### 2. EDITORIAL LAYOUT MANDATE
* **Visual Structure:** Use **Markdown**. Use `# H1` for Phase Titles and `### H3` for Technical Insights.
* **Placeholder System:** Interperse clearly labeled placeholders for images of varying sizes (e.g., `[FIGURE 01: FULL-PAGE EDITORIAL]`, `[FIGURE 02: ASYMMETRIC INSET]`, `[FIGURE 03: TECHNICAL MACRO]`).
* **The "Tanah & Laut" Palette:** In text descriptions for headers or accents, reference colors like **Tanah Vulkanik** (dark earth), **Pasir Kabut** (warm sand), and **Benang Merah** (accent red).
* **Tone:** Removed, authoritative, and sophisticated. Strip all "AI-isms" (e.g., "I can help with that," "Certainly"). 

#### 3. CONTENT TRANSFORMATION
* **The Inquiry:** Present the original "User Prompts" as *Editorial Inquiries* in bold italics.
* **The Blueprint:** Present the "AI Responses" as *Strategic Directives* or *Lead Features*.

#### 4. THE IMAGE GENERATION APPENDIX
At the end of the document, generate a separate sheet of **12 specific Image Generation Prompts** (optimized for Midjourney/DALL-E) that correspond to the placeholders. They must focus on:
* **Subject:** Architectural concrete, macro textile weaves, high-fashion minimalist posing, and blueprint-style sketches.
* **Lighting:** Sharp, geometric shadows and soft, natural gallery light.

**Action:** Begin with **PHASE I: THE SOVEREIGN IDENTITY**. Generate the layout and content for the naming and persona strategy.

### How to use this strategy:
1.  **Step 1:** Give the prompt above to your primary writing model (e.g., Claude 3.5 Sonnet or Gemini 1.5 Pro). 
2.  **Step 2:** Once it generates the text and the "Image Appendix," take the specific image prompts to a generator (like Midjourney) to create the visuals.
3.  **Step 3:** Manually replace the `[FIGURE XX]` placeholders in your Markdown editor with your generated images for a final, print-ready PDF.
